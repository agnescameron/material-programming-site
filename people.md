## [claire anderson]()
Claire is a textile designer and researcher, and the course leader for BA Textile Design at Chelsea College of Arts. She is currently undertaking a practice-based PhD at the London College of Fashion under the supervision of Professor Veronika Kapsali and Dr Mingjing Lin. Her research is concerned with the relationship between colour and textiles, democratised textile technologies, and responsible textile and material futures.


## [b claxton](https://www.instagram.com/b.clax)

B Claxton is a knitwear designer whose practise spans freelance work with fashion and textile brands alongside academic and technical roles within higher education. Their expertise focuses on knit development, and programming, including Shima Seiki and smart textile applications. They are senior lecturer for Smart and Digital Textile Design at Chelsea College of Arts.


## [agnes cameron](https://agnescameron.info/)

Agnes is a hardware and software engineer with an interest in open-source collective knowledge production. She works as a specialist technician and lecturer at the UAL Creative Computing Institute (CCI).
