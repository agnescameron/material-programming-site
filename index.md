This is the homepage for the **Material Programming Research Project**, a collaboration between researchers at UAL's Chelsea College of Arts and Creative Computing Institute.

We are working with and on new open-source programming tools for the development of knitted textiles. We're interested in working with domestic and semi-industrial machines, [malleable](https://www.inkandswitch.com/malleable-software/) textile design software, and researchers and practicioners across industry and academia.

For **Spring and Summer 2026**, we are running a series of **[research talks](/talks)** on knitting technology, and **[workshops](/workshops)** with students on making and using open-source knitting software.

If any of the above sounds interesting to you, please <a href="mailto:a.cameron@arts.ac.uk">get in touch</a>.
