material programming research project

This is the homepage for the Material Programming Research Project, a collaboration between researchers at UAL's Chelsea College of Art and Design and Creative Computing Institute. We are working on new open-source programming tools for the development of knitted textiles. We're interested in working with domestic and semi-industrial machines, design software that uses code, and researchers and practicioners across industry and academia.

If any of the above sounds interesting to you, please get in touch.
