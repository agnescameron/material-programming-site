Gerard Rubio is the co-founder of [Kniterate](https://www.kniterate.com/). Kniterate is a compact digital knitting machine, which has a strong ethos of community and has an open-source approach to programming, knitting, and making.

He discussed the roots of the company in design research, and the future of the Kniterate platform.
