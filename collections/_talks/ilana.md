[Ilana Blumberg](https://ilanablumberg.co.uk/) is an independent knitwear designer and STOLL programmer. Alongside having her own knitwear brand she has worked for many fashion houses including Chopova Lowena, Sinéad O'Dwyer, Harry Styles, Knwls and Dilara Findikoglu.

Ilana talked about her practice, and her extensive work learning and programming the Stoll machine. She had brought a number of samples to show the students how 3D forms can be created through custom design.
